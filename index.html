About Me

Hello World! I'm An, an energy engineer turned tech enthusiast, and I'm here to bridge the gap between the world of energy and the boundless possibilities of technology. My background in energy engineering taught me the art of understanding intricate systems. Now, I'm channeling that same mindset into crafting digital landscapes. This journey isn't about abandoning one field for another; it's about connecting the dots between my past and my future, between energy and technology. Whether it's building dynamic websites, creating interactive web applications, or bringing innovative ideas to life,I'm looking looking forward to new challenges and this site is my digital journal of exploration and growth. Within this virtual space, you'll discover my explorations, experiments, and collaborations. Each project is a microcosm of my journey, an insight into the way I approach challenges, and a glimpse into my evolving perspective on technology.



Consider this portfolio an ongoing narrative, a reflection of my curiosity-driven evolution. Whether you're a fellow traveler, an industry veteran, or simply curious about this crossroads, I invite you to join me in exploring the fusion of worlds, one line of code at a time.
